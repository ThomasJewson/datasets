Dataset:  wage_cpi.csv

Source: I. Dolca and M. Nicolov (2013). "Analysis of Relationship Between
Net Wage and Consumer Price Index," Procedia Economics and Finance, Vol. 6,
pp. 738-747.

Description: Median net wage (X) and Consumer Price Index (Y) for Romania
for months January 2000-September 2010.

Variables/Labels
Year     (year)
Month of year   (yr_month)
Series Month    (ser_month)
Median Salary   (wage)
Consumer Price Index   (cpi)
